Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes and Ham

3 tablespoons butter 1 small yellow onion, diced 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1 cup half-and-half ½ cup whole milk salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 teaspoon garlic powder ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce ½ teaspoon onion powder ¼ teaspoon paprika 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced 1 ½ cups chopped hickory smoked ham ½ cup grated Cheddar cheese ½ cup grated Monterey Jack cheese
